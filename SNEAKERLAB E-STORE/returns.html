Policy & Satisfaction

Returns &
Exchanges

We stand behind our collection. If your pair isn't perfect, we've designed a straightforward process to get it right.

The Guarantee

You have 3 days from the date of delivery to initiate a return or exchange.

All items must be returned in Deadstock Condition—unworn, with original packaging fully intact.

Final Sale Items

Pre-ordered items and limited release "Hype" drops are eligible for exchange only, no refunds.

How to Return

01

Initiate Request

Contact our support team with your order number and reason for return.

02

Verification

Wait for a return authorization. We usually respond within 24 hours.

03

Secure Shipping

Pack the sneakers securely in a double-box to protect the original sneaker box. Once you are ready to ship your return, address your package to shipping address which will be provided by our support team.

04

Return Shipping Method

Use a trackable and insured shipping method to return your package, with your order number clearly printed on your shipping label.

05

Refund Processing

Once your return is authenticated, please allow 24-72 hours for your bank to credit the refund back to your original payment method.

06

Final Notification

You will receive a final confirmation email once the transaction is completed on our end.

Important Notice

Sneaker Lab is not responsible for any return shipping fees, or for returns that are lost or stolen while in transit. We strongly recommend using a trackable and insured shipping method with a signature requirement.
