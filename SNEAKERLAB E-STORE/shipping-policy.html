Shipping Logistics

From Us to your destination. We provide secure, authenticated shipping for every single order. Delivering nationwide, our goal is to offer you the best shipping options, no matter where you live. Every day, we deliver to our customers across Zimbabwe, ensuring that we provide the very highest levels of responsiveness to you at all times.

Rates & Timelines

The time frame for order delivery is divided into two parts - Processing Time & Shipping Time:

Processing Time

Please allow 24-48 hours for your order to be processed and shipped. This includes Order verification, tailoring, quality check and packaging.

Shipping Time

After processing and leaving our warehouse, orders usually take between 1-2 business days to arrive at their destination but can take longer from time to time, depending on shipping delays which are out of our control but you will be notified if there are any delays. This mainly depends on your location, you may receive your order earlier. You will also receive a shipment notification with tracking once your order has been shipped. Please allow up to 24 hours after placing order for your tracking information to become active and updated.
